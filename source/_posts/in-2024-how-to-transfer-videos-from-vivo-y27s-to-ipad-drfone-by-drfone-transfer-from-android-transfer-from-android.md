### Method 1: Move to iOS App

The Move to iOS app stands as an official app from Apple. This app is tailor-made to simplify the transition from Vivo Y27s to iOS devices, streamlining the migration process. It efficiently transfers crucial data such as videos, contacts, messages, photos, and more. Moreover, utilizing Move to iOS is a straightforward procedure.

- **Step 3.** Input the code and wait until the connection is established between both devices. After the transfer screen emerges on the Android device, select the desired videos and other data for transfer, then tap "Continue."
