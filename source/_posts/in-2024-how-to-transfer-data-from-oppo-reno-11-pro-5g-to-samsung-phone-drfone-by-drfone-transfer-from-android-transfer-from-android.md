## Part 1. How to Transfer Content from Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G to iPhone Directly

You must be familiar with Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G Migrate if you are a Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G user. It is a solution for seamless data transfer used in Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G devices. With this app, you can transfer data from your Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G device to others, such as iPhones.

### Solution 1. Using ShareIt App

When Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G Migrate is not an option, ShareIt can be a versatile alternative for **Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G to iPhone transfer**. ShareIt is a popular cross-platform file-sharing application known for its simplicity and efficiency. To use it, you'll need to download the ShareIt app on both your Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G and iPhone.

If you have downloaded and installed it, you can continue with the steps below:

- **Step 1:** Connect your Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G and iPhone devices

Since ShareIt operates by creating a hotspot network among devices, it's necessary to have both devices connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Make sure to keep both devices close to each other, too.

- **Step 2:** Select the files to transfer

On your Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G device, click the "Send" button. After a new window appears displaying all the categorized files on your device, choose the specific files you want to transfer.

- **Step 3:** Look for the Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G device to transfer

After selecting the files, press "Send" and provide the necessary permissions for GPS and Bluetooth. Once permissions are granted, the Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G device will initiate a search for nearby devices.

- **Step 4:** Transfer the files to the iPhone.

On your iPhone, open the ShareIt app and click "Receive." This action opens all the necessary ports to receive shared files. When your Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G phone detects your iPhone, tap on the iPhone’s avatar to proceed with the transfer.

### Solution 2. Move Contents from Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G to iPhone via Apps & Data

Another good alternative of **Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G to iPhone transfer app** is Move to iOS. This Move to iOS app is designed by Apple specifically for users moving from Android devices to iPhones. However, if you have been using your iPhone for a while, you must reset your iPhone first to proceed with this method. It can be done by going to Settings > General > Reset > Erase all content and settings. After resetting, your iPhone will be going back to the initial setup.

- **Step 1:** Setup your iPhone

- Open Move to iOS on your Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G phone. Review the terms and conditions and press "Agree" to proceed.

- **Step 3:** Enter the Code
- On your Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G, a screen will appear to request a 6-10-digit code. This code will be generated on your iPhone. Enter the displayed code on your Oppo Reno 11 Pro 5G device to make a secure connection between the two devices.

- **Step 4:** Select Content to Transfer
- Next, choose the types of content you wish to transfer. Tap "Continue" to initiate the transfer process. Remember to keep your devices close to each other and connected to power until the transfer process is completed for quicker transfer.

- **Step 5:** Transfer complete.
- Once the transfer is complete, you will be prompted to finish setting up your iPhone. Follow the instructions to complete the setup process.
