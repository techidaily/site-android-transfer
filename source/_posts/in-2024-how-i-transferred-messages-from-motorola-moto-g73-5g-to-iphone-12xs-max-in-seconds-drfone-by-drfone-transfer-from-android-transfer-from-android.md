If you have downloaded and installed it, you can continue with the steps below:

- **Step 1:** Connect your Motorola Moto G73 5G and iPhone devices

- **Step 2:** Select the files to transfer

On your Motorola Moto G73 5G device, click the "Send" button. After a new window appears displaying all the categorized files on your device, choose the specific files you want to transfer.

- **Step 3:** Look for the Motorola Moto G73 5G device to transfer

- **Step 4:** Transfer the files to the iPhone.

On your iPhone, open the ShareIt app and click "Receive." This action opens all the necessary ports to receive shared files. When your Motorola Moto G73 5G phone detects your iPhone, tap on the iPhone’s avatar to proceed with the transfer.
