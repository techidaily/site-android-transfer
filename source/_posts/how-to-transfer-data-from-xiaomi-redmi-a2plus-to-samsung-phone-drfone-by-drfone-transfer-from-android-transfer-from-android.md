## Part 1. How to Transfer Content from Xiaomi Redmi A2+ to iPhone Directly

You must be familiar with Xiaomi Redmi A2+ Migrate if you are a Xiaomi Redmi A2+ user. It is a solution for seamless data transfer used in Xiaomi Redmi A2+ devices. With this app, you can transfer data from your Xiaomi Redmi A2+ device to others, such as iPhones.

### Solution 1. Using ShareIt App

When Xiaomi Redmi A2+ Migrate is not an option, ShareIt can be a versatile alternative for **Xiaomi Redmi A2+ to iPhone transfer**. ShareIt is a popular cross-platform file-sharing application known for its simplicity and efficiency. To use it, you'll need to download the ShareIt app on both your Xiaomi Redmi A2+ and iPhone.

If you have downloaded and installed it, you can continue with the steps below:

- **Step 1:** Connect your Xiaomi Redmi A2+ and iPhone devices

Since ShareIt operates by creating a hotspot network among devices, it's necessary to have both devices connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Make sure to keep both devices close to each other, too.

- **Step 2:** Select the files to transfer

On your Xiaomi Redmi A2+ device, click the "Send" button. After a new window appears displaying all the categorized files on your device, choose the specific files you want to transfer.

- **Step 3:** Look for the Xiaomi Redmi A2+ device to transfer

After selecting the files, press "Send" and provide the necessary permissions for GPS and Bluetooth. Once permissions are granted, the Xiaomi Redmi A2+ device will initiate a search for nearby devices.

- **Step 4:** Transfer the files to the iPhone.

On your iPhone, open the ShareIt app and click "Receive." This action opens all the necessary ports to receive shared files. When your Xiaomi Redmi A2+ phone detects your iPhone, tap on the iPhone’s avatar to proceed with the transfer.
