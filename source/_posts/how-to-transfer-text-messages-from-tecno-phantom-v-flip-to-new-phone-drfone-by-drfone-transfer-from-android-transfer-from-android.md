**Here is how to transfer messages from Tecno Phantom V Flip to iPhone 12/XS (Max) with SMS Backup+:**

1. Get the Android phone and sign in to your ‘Gmail account’ and press ‘Settings’. Go to ‘Forwarding and POP/IMAP’. Now, tap ‘Enable IMAP’ and click ‘Save Changes’.
2. Download SMS Backup+ on your Tecno Phantom V Flip from Google Play Store and launch it. Tap ‘Connect’ choose the recently used Gmail account. Now, allow the app to backup the SMS to your Gmail account and hit ‘Backup’.
