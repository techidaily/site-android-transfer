## Part 1. How to Transfer Content from Motorola Razr 40 to iPhone Directly

You must be familiar with Motorola Razr 40 Migrate if you are a Motorola Razr 40 user. It is a solution for seamless data transfer used in Motorola Razr 40 devices. With this app, you can transfer data from your Motorola Razr 40 device to others, such as iPhones.

### Solution 1. Using ShareIt App

When Motorola Razr 40 Migrate is not an option, ShareIt can be a versatile alternative for **Motorola Razr 40 to iPhone transfer**. ShareIt is a popular cross-platform file-sharing application known for its simplicity and efficiency. To use it, you'll need to download the ShareIt app on both your Motorola Razr 40 and iPhone.

If you have downloaded and installed it, you can continue with the steps below:

- **Step 1:** Connect your Motorola Razr 40 and iPhone devices

Since ShareIt operates by creating a hotspot network among devices, it's necessary to have both devices connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Make sure to keep both devices close to each other, too.

- **Step 2:** Select the files to transfer

On your Motorola Razr 40 device, click the "Send" button. After a new window appears displaying all the categorized files on your device, choose the specific files you want to transfer.

- **Step 3:** Look for the Motorola Razr 40 device to transfer

After selecting the files, press "Send" and provide the necessary permissions for GPS and Bluetooth. Once permissions are granted, the Motorola Razr 40 device will initiate a search for nearby devices.

- **Step 4:** Transfer the files to the iPhone.

On your iPhone, open the ShareIt app and click "Receive." This action opens all the necessary ports to receive shared files. When your Motorola Razr 40 phone detects your iPhone, tap on the iPhone’s avatar to proceed with the transfer.
