## Part 3. How to transfer music from Vivo V29 Pro to Other Android Devices using Bluetooth?

**Here are the steps to follow to know how to transfer music from Vivo V29 Pro to Other Android Devices.**

**Step 1.** There are two ways you can turn on Bluetooth on your Vivo V29 Pro

**Method 1:** The first method is to swipe from top to down on your Vivo V29 Pro to view the swipe menu on some Android OS. You would be able to view and immediately turn on Bluetooth with a single click.

**Method 2:** Go to “Connection” from the Settings Menu on your Vivo V29 Pro, and then in the Connections options, you will see “Bluetooth”. Make sure it is switched on. Also, ensure that the phone’s Bluetooth visibility is enabled so that your device can be seen and easily paired with the other device.

Mostly, you will be provided with a pair confirmation code that will be displayed on both devices. Click OK to pair both devices successfully.

![transfer music from android to android-pair both devices](https://images.wondershare.com/drfone/article/2018/06/transfer-music-from-android-to-android-9.jpg)

**Step 3.** The final step is to head to the File Manager app on your phone or go to your Music Player, select the music file you wish to transfer, and then click on your device's Share button or logo.

Here, scroll until you see the “Bluetooth” option. You will be immediately prompted to select the Vivo V29 Pro device to share with, click the earlier paired device name, and then click on “Accept” on the other device.

**Step 1.** First, enable NFC connection on both the Vivo V29 Pro devices between which you want to transfer the music files.
