If you have downloaded and installed it, you can continue with the steps below:

- **Step 1:** Connect your Oppo A18 and iPhone devices

- **Step 2:** Select the files to transfer

On your Oppo A18 device, click the "Send" button. After a new window appears displaying all the categorized files on your device, choose the specific files you want to transfer.

- **Step 4:** Transfer the files to the iPhone.

On your iPhone, open the ShareIt app and click "Receive." This action opens all the necessary ports to receive shared files. When your Oppo A18 phone detects your iPhone, tap on the iPhone’s avatar to proceed with the transfer.
