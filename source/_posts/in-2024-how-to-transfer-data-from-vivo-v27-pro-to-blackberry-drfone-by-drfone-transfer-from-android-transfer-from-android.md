After pressing OK, swipe left. A new screen will show up. You will have the option to choose the Vivo V27 Pro device you are switching data from. Make sure to click Android.

![transfer data from Vivo V27 Pro to BlackBerry-06](https://images.wondershare.com/drfone/others/how-to-transfer-data-from-android-to-blackberry05.jpg)
