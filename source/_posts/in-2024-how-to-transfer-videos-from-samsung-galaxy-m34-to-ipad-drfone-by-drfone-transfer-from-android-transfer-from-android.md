### Method 1: Move to iOS App

The Move to iOS app stands as an official app from Apple. This app is tailor-made to simplify the transition from Samsung Galaxy M34 to iOS devices, streamlining the migration process. It efficiently transfers crucial data such as videos, contacts, messages, photos, and more. Moreover, utilizing Move to iOS is a straightforward procedure.

- **Step 3.** Input the code and wait until the connection is established between both devices.
