## How to Transfer Content from android to iPhone Directly

Since Xiaomi 14 is embracing Android as its operating system, transitioning from an iPhone to a Xiaomi 14, or vice versa, can pose challenges due to the differing operating systems. But it doesn't mean impossible!

If you're switching to an iPhone and need to transfer all your data, this article has you covered. We will look at ways to facilitate smooth **Xiaomi 14 to iPhone transfer** while ensuring your valuable information transfers across these devices with no problem.

## Part 1. How to Transfer Content from Xiaomi 14 to iPhone Directly

You must be familiar with Xiaomi 14 Migrate if you are a Xiaomi 14 user. It is a solution for seamless data transfer used in Xiaomi 14 devices. With this app, you can transfer data from your Xiaomi 14 device to others, such as iPhones.

However, most of the latest Xiaomi 14 models are now unable to run Xiaomi 14 Migrate since it's a pretty outdated app. It has been retired by the company since 2015. Users are seeking alternative methods for data transfer as a result. In this section, we explore more effective solutions to transfer content directly from Xiaomi 14 to iPhone besides Xiaomi 14 Migrate.

### Solution 1. Using ShareIt App

When Xiaomi 14 Migrate is not an option, ShareIt can be a versatile alternative for **Xiaomi 14 to iPhone transfer**. ShareIt is a popular cross-platform file-sharing application known for its simplicity and efficiency. To use it, you'll need to download the ShareIt app on both your Xiaomi 14 and iPhone.

If you have downloaded and installed it, you can continue with the steps below:

- **Step 1:** Connect your Xiaomi 14 and iPhone devices

Since ShareIt operates by creating a hotspot network among devices, it's necessary to have both devices connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Make sure to keep both devices close to each other, too.

- **Step 2:** Select the files to transfer

On your Xiaomi 14 device, click the "Send" button. After a new window appears displaying all the categorized files on your device, choose the specific files you want to transfer.

- **Step 3:** Look for the Xiaomi 14 device to transfer

After selecting the files, press "Send" and provide the necessary permissions for GPS and Bluetooth. Once permissions are granted, the Xiaomi 14 device will initiate a search for nearby devices.

- **Step 4:** Transfer the files to the iPhone.

On your iPhone, open the ShareIt app and click "Receive." This action opens all the necessary ports to receive shared files. When your Xiaomi 14 phone detects your iPhone, tap on the iPhone’s avatar to proceed with the transfer.

![Xiaomi 14 to iPhone transfer with ShareIt.](https://images.wondershare.com/drfone/article/2024/01/transfer-data-from-motorola-to-iphone-1.jpg)

### Solution 2. Move Contents from Xiaomi 14 to iPhone via Apps & Data

You can download it from the Google Play Store. Then, you can transfer the data by accessing Apps & Data on the iPhone.

However, if you have been using your iPhone for a while, you must reset your iPhone first to proceed with this method. It can be done by going to Settings > General > Reset > Erase all content and settings. After resetting, your iPhone will be going back to the initial setup.

For more details, follow the instructions below:

- **Step 1:** Setup your iPhone

- **Step 4:** Select Content to Transfer

- **Step 5:** Transfer complete.
- Once the transfer is complete, you will be prompted to finish setting up your iPhone. Follow the instructions to complete the setup process.
